Our Dental Billing Process

Our streamlined process ensures that your practice's billing is efficient, transparent, and error-free. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Patient Information Collection

We gather essential patient information, including insurance details and demographics, to ensure the billing process runs smoothly.

Step 2: Code Assignment

Our certified coders assign accurate codes for dental procedures to ensure that claims are submitted correctly and payments are maximized.

Step 3: Claim Submission

We submit claims to insurance companies on behalf of your practice, ensuring that all information is accurate and compliant with industry standards.

Step 4: Payment Posting

Once payment is received, we post the payments accurately to patient accounts and update your records to ensure everything is in order.

Step 5: Patient Billing

We handle patient billing for any remaining balances, ensuring clear communication and ease of payment through various channels.

Step 6: Denial Management

We manage and resolve denied claims, ensuring that any issues are addressed promptly and efficiently, reducing the time between service and payment.
